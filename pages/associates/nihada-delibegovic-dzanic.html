Nihada Delibegović Džanić

Cognitive Linguistics, Phraseology, Political Humor

Full Professor at the University of Tuzla - Visiting Professor at the Alpe Adria University in Klagenfurt, Austria

Biography

Nihada Delibegović Džanić, PhD, is a Full Professor of English Language and Linguistics at the University of Tuzla, Bosnia and Herzegovina, where she teaches linguistics courses at both the undergraduate and postgraduate levels. She is also a visiting professor at the Alpe Adria University in Klagenfurt, Austria. Her main research interests are cognitive linguistics, ELT methodology and phraseology. She has authored and co-authored more than fifty papers and has participated in numerous international conferences.
