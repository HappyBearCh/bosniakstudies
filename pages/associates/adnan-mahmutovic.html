Adnan Mahmutović

English Literature

Stockholm University

Biography

Adnan Mahmutović is a writer and a Professor of English Literature at Stockholm University. His creative work includes two novels and a collection of short stories: Thinner than a Hair (Cinnamon Press, 2010); At the Feet of Mothers (Cinnamon Press, 2020); How to Fare Well and Stay Fair (Salt Publishing, 2012). His academic work includes Ways of Being Free (Rodopi, 2012); Visions of the Future in Comics (McFarland Press, 2017); The Craft of Editing (Routledge, 2018); Claiming Space: Locations and Orientations in World Literatures (Bloomsbury, 2021).
