İbrahim Fevzi Güven

Political Science and International Relations

Assistant Professor, Karabuk University, Turkey

Biography

Ibrahim Fevzi Guven is a political scientist and lecturer in the Department of International Relations at Karabuk University, Türkiye. His research focuses on peace and conflict studies, the local turn in peacebuilding, post-conflict societies, everyday peace, nationalism, and security studies. He has conducted several field studies in Bosnia and Herzegovina and has published widely on the region. From 2024 to 2025, he was a visiting scholar at the Carter School for Peace and Conflict Resolution, where he conducted a research project on everyday peace and the interpretation of the past in post-conflict Bosnia and Herzegovina.
