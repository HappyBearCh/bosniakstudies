Senad Jaskić, DC, MAcOM, MS, LAc

Chiropractic Physician and Licensed Acupuncturist at Coatesville VA Hospital

Biography

Dr. Senad Jaskic is a chiropractic physician and a licensed acupuncturist at Coatesville Veterans Affairs Hospital in Coatesville, Pennsylvania. He specializes in pain and neuro-musculoskeletal rehabilitation. He earned his Doctor of Chiropractic degree from Logan University, Master of Acupuncture and Oriental Medicine from American College of Acupuncture and Oriental Medicine, and MS in Nutrition and Human Performance from Logan University. Dr. Jaskic is also certified in Performance Health Rehabilitation, Kinesio Taping, Dry Needling, and is a Diplomate in Acupuncture (NCCAOM). He is a double board-licensed Chiropractic Physician, and Licensed Acupuncturist in the state of Tennessee. He is also a former faculty member and clinical supervisor at East West College of Natural Medicine in Sarasota, Florida. He was born in Kozarac, Bosnia and Herzegovina, and like many other Bosnians, is a genocide survivor. His interests besides chiropractic and Chinese medicine include Islamic and Sunnah studies, reading, hiking, traveling, soccer, nutrition, and fitness.
