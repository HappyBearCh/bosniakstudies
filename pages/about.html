About Bosniak Studies

Dedicated to exploring the past, present, and future of Bosniak communities worldwide

Who We Are

Bosniak Studies is a leading academic platform dedicated to the scholarly exploration of Bosniak communities worldwide. We bring together researchers, scholars, and community members in a collaborative effort to advance knowledge about Bosniak history, culture, society and other related fields.

Our organization spans across multiple countries and disciplines while focusing on Bosniak people in Bosnia and Herzegovina, Sandžak, Kosovo, Macedonia, Türkiye, and Bosniak diaspora communities globally. We believe in the power of rigorous scholarship to preserve heritage and foster understanding.

Our Values

Academic Excellence

We are committed to the highest standards of scholarly research and analysis, ensuring accuracy and depth in all our work.

Cultural Preservation

We work to preserve and celebrate the rich cultural heritage of the Bosniak people for current and future generations.

Global Dialogue

We foster meaningful conversation among scholars and community members across continents and cultures.

Truth and Integrity

Following the wisdom of Hasan Kafi al-Aqhisari, we pursue truth with sincerity and integrity in all endeavors.

Areas of Focus

History & Heritage

Exploring the rich historical narrative spanning from ancient times to the contemporary period

Culture & Traditions

Celebrating and documenting the unique cultural expressions and traditions of Bosniak communities

Language & Literature

Studying the Bosnian language and its literary contributions to global culture

Religion & Philosophy

Examining the philosophical and religious contributions of Bosniak thinkers and scholars

Contemporary Society

Understanding modern Bosniak communities and their role in contemporary global society

Global Diaspora

Researching Bosniak diaspora communities and their integration and contributions worldwide
