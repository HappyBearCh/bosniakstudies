Our Mission

Bosniak Studies aims to become a leader in the academic exploration of all issues relevant to Bosniaks in Bosnia and Herzegovina, Sandžak, Kosovo, Macedonia, Türkiye, and the global diaspora.

Our mission is to foster scholarly interest, generate and disseminate knowledge about Bosniak individuals and communities, including their culture, traditions, history, language, and contributions to global society.
